ESPORTS AND COMPETATIVE GAMES

Esports titles like League of Legends, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, and Overwatch prioritize high frame rates and responsiveness. To excel in these games, a gaming PC with a mid-range to high-end CPU, a dedicated graphics card (e.g., GEN-Z Z3060 or higher), at least 16 GB of RAM, and an SSD would be recommended. A monitor with a high refresh rate (e.g., 144 Hz) would also enhance the gaming experience.

VIRTUAL REALITY (VR) GAMING

VR gaming requires a robust system to render the virtual environment in real-time. In addition to the requirements for triple-A games, VR gaming typically benefits from even more powerful hardware. Look for a high-end CPU (e.g., GEN-Z z9), a top-tier graphics card (e.g., GEN-Z Z3080 or higher), 32 GB or more of RAM, and a spacious SSD for quick loading times. You'll also need a VR headset compatible with your PC, such as the Oculus Rift or HTC Vive.
